MUSIC
INGAJA
I’m INGAJA. Living here, by the Baltic Sea in Lithuania. There was quite a lot of music present in my life. Some I inhaled from others, particularly Ben Howard, Lissie, and RY X. Some I exhaled myself, breathing it out with my own distinctive sounds. I infuse my melodies with deep dark indie and acoustic rock tinges. Most of all I love to savor the moments when these melodies embrace the concert venues. That is why I am always willing to perform, though I also record my music. In February 2017 I collected them all in my first EP album
D A R K N E S S. Some of them got broadcasted on BBC radio in Liverpool. I’m happy to have you here. Welcome.
